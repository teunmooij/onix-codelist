ONIX Code Lists Issue 58, July 2022

List 167: Price condition type

Value Description Notes
00 No conditions Allows positive indication that there are no conditions (the default if <PriceCondition> is omitted)
01 Includes updates Purchase at this price includes specified updates
02 Must also purchase updates Purchase at this price requires commitment to purchase specified updates, not included in price
03 Updates available Updates may be purchased separately, no minimum commitment required
04 Linked subsequent purchase price Use with <PriceConditionQuantity> and <ProductIdentifier>. Purchase at this price requires commitment to purchase the specified linked product, which is not included in the price
05 Linked prior purchase price Use with <PriceConditionQuantity> and <ProductIdentifier>. Purchase at this price requires prior purchase of the specified linked product
06 Linked price Use with <PriceConditionQuantity> and <ProductIdentifier>. Purchase at this price requires simultaneous purchase of the specified linked product, which is not included in the price
07 Auto-renewing The rental or subscription will automatically renew at the end of the period unless actively cancelled
08 Combined price Purchase at this price includes the price of the specified other product
10 Rental duration The duration of the rental to which the price applies. Deprecated, use <PriceConstraint> instead
11 Rental to purchase Purchase at this price requires prior rental of the product. <PriceConditionQuantity> gives minimum prior rental period, and <ProductIdentifier> may be used if rental uses a different product identifier
12 Rental extension Upgrade to longer rental duration. <PriceConditionQuantity> gives minimum prior rental duration, and <ProductIdentifier> may be used if rental uses a different product identifier. Separate price constraint with time limited license duration (code 07) specifies the new combined rental duration
