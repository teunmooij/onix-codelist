ONIX Code Lists Issue 58, July 2022

List 141: Barcode indicator

Value Description Notes
00 Not barcoded
01 Barcoded, scheme unspecified
02 GTIN-13 Barcode uses 13-digit EAN symbology (without 5-digit extension)
03 GTIN-13+5 (US dollar price encoded) EAN symbology, first digit of 5-digit extension is 1–5
04 GTIN-13+5 (CAN dollar price encoded) EAN symbology, first digit of 5-digit extension is 6
05 GTIN-13+5 (no price encoded) EAN symbology, 5-digit extension is 90000, or extension does not indicate a price
06 UPC-12 (item-specific) AKA item/price
07 UPC-12+5 (item-specific) AKA item/price
08 UPC-12 (price-point) AKA price/item
09 UPC-12+5 (price-point) AKA price/item
10 GTIN-13+5 (UK Pound Sterling price encoded) EAN symbology, first digit of 5-digit extension is 0
